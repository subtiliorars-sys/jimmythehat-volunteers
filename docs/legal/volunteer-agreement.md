# Volunteer Agreement

**JimmyTheHat Games - Volunteer Program**

---

## Purpose

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions for volunteering with JimmyTheHat Games.

## Nature of Relationship

**Volunteer Status:** This is a volunteer/educational opportunity. There is no expectation of compensation, and this is not an employment relationship.

**Educational Focus:** The primary purpose is to provide learning opportunities and portfolio-building experience for volunteers.

## Responsibilities

### Volunteer Responsibilities
- Contribute 5-10 hours per week as agreed
- Communicate reliably and respond to messages within 48 hours
- Follow project guidelines and code of conduct
- Complete assigned tasks in a timely manner
- Participate in code reviews and feedback sessions
- Respect confidentiality of project information

### Project Responsibilities
- Provide mentorship and guidance
- Offer learning opportunities and feedback
- Maintain a respectful and inclusive environment
- Provide documentation of volunteer hours (if needed for school credit)
- Recognize volunteer contributions appropriately

## Intellectual Property

**Contributions:** All volunteer contributions are made under the project's open source license.

**Ownership:** Volunteers retain ownership of their contributions, but grant the project the right to use, modify, and distribute the contributions under the project's license.

**Credit:** Volunteers will be credited for their contributions in the project's documentation and release notes.

## Code of Conduct

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Communicate professionally
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Focus on constructive feedback
- Collaborate and share knowledge

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Disruptive behavior
- Sharing confidential information

### Reporting
Report code of conduct violations to the project lead via email or private message.

## Termination

**By Volunteer:** Volunteers may leave at any time with no notice required.

**By Project:** The project may terminate the volunteer relationship if:
- Code of conduct is violated
- Reliability issues persist
- Communication is consistently poor
- The volunteer is no longer a good fit for the project

## Liability

**Waiver:** Volunteers participate at their own risk. JimmyTheHat Games is not liable for any injuries, damages, or losses incurred during volunteer activities.

**Insurance:** Volunteers are encouraged to have their own insurance coverage.

## Confidentiality

**Project Information:** Volunteers may have access to non-public project information. This information must not be shared outside the project without explicit permission.

**Personal Information:** Volunteer personal information will be kept confidential and used only for project purposes.

## Duration

This agreement is effective from the date of acceptance and continues until terminated by either party.

## Acknowledgment

By participating in the volunteer program, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms.
