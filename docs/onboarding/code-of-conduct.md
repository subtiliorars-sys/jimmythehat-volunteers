# Code of Conduct

**JimmyTheHat Games - Volunteer Program**

---

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful:** Treat everyone with respect and kindness
- **Be Inclusive:** Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- **Be Collaborative:** Work together and share knowledge
- **Be Professional:** Communicate clearly and meet commitments
- **Focus on What's Best for the Community:** Focus on improving the project
- **Show Empathy:** Consider different perspectives and experiences

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment:** Unwelcome comments, jokes, or advances
- **Discriminatory Language:** Slurs, stereotypes, or exclusionary language
- **Personal Attacks:** Insults, name-calling, or ad hominem attacks
- **Trolling:** Deliberately provoking or disrupting discussions
- **Spam:** Unwanted or repetitive messages
- **Sharing Private Information:** Posting others' private information without permission
- **Other Unprofessional Conduct:** Any behavior that creates a hostile environment

## Reporting

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please:

1. **Contact the Project Lead**
   - Email: volunteers@jimmythehat.com
   - Include details of the incident
   - If possible, include screenshots or logs

2. **What to Include**
   - Your contact information
   - Names of those involved
   - Description of the incident
   - Any relevant evidence

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible. We will protect the identity of reporters when appropriate.

## Response

### What Happens After Reporting

1. **Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge receipt within 24-48 hours
2. **Investigation:** We will investigate the incident thoroughly
3. **Action:** We will take appropriate action based on the investigation
4. **Follow-up:** We will follow up with the reporter as appropriate

### Possible Actions

- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from the project
- Reporting to platform administrators (if applicable)
- Legal action (if necessary)

## For Volunteers Under 18

### Additional Protections

- **No One-on-One Contact:** All communication should be in public channels or with multiple participants
- **Parental Involvement:** Parents can request access to communication logs
- **Age-Appropriate Content:** All content and discussions should be age-appropriate
- **Supervision:** Increased supervision and mentorship for younger volunteers

### Reporting for Minors

If you are under 18 and experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Tell a Parent/Guardian:** Inform your parent or guardian
2. **Report to Project:** Report to the project lead (with parent if possible)
3. **Document:** Keep records of the incident if safe to do so

## Enforcement

### Who Enforces This Code of Conduct

The project lead and designated moderators are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct.

### Appeal Process

If you believe action taken against you is unfair:

1. **Submit an Appeal:** Email volunteers@jimmythehat.com with your appeal
2. **Include Details:** Explain why you believe the action was unfair
3. **Review:** The appeal will be reviewed by the project lead
4. **Decision:** You will be informed of the decision

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- **All Project Spaces:** GitHub repositories, Discord servers, email communications
- **All Participants:** Volunteers, mentors, project leads, and community members
- **All Events:** Online meetings, game jams, and other project events
